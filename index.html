Background

About the Conference

The Centre for Teaching, Learning and Development (CTLD) is entrusted with the responsibility of strengthening student training, employability, faculty capability, and holistic development at Teerthanker Mahaveer University. In alignment with the National Education Policy (NEP) 2020, rapid digital transformation in education, and global shifts in workforce expectations, there is a compelling need for scholarly engagement on education, skills, pedagogy, and employability.

It is proposed that CTLD organize an International Online Conference to invite research papers related to training, pedagogy, employability, and student development. The conference will be conducted completely online via an approved digital platform.

6 Research Tracks
5 Advisory Members
2 Conference Days

This conference will enhance TMU's global academic reputation and strengthen CTLD's training profile.
