Background · CTLD 2026

About the
Conference

The Centre for Teaching, Learning and Development (CTLD) is entrusted with strengthening student training, employability, faculty capability, and holistic development at Teerthanker Mahaveer University. In alignment with NEP 2020, rapid digital transformation in education, and global shifts in workforce expectations, there is a compelling need for scholarly engagement on education, skills, pedagogy, and employability.

CTLD proposes an International Online Conference to invite research papers related to training, pedagogy, employability, and student development — conducted completely online via an approved digital platform.
